The Science

What are
plant sterols?

Plant sterols, also called phytosterols, are naturally found in all plants. They are the plant equivalent of human cholesterol — an integral building block of plant cell walls.

Their molecular similarity to human cholesterol is precisely what gives them their powerful therapeutic effect. IDHMA sources, processes, and supplies these compounds to exacting specifications.

01
Clinically proven cholesterol reduction

Reduces LDL cholesterol by competing with it for intestinal absorption. Incorporated into heart-healthy functional foods — spreads, juices, milk, yogurt — sold worldwide.

02
Primary pharma precursor

Phytosterols are the starting material for steroid hormone medicines including corticosteroids, contraceptives, and anabolic compounds.

03
GRAS status — naturally safe

Generally Recognised As Safe. Naturally present in vegetable oils, nuts, seeds, and whole grains consumed daily around the world.

04
100% plant-based, fully traceable

Extracted entirely from natural plant-based feedstocks with full traceability from source through to finished specification.
